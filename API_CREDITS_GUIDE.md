# API Credits Guide

## Current Error
You're seeing: "Your credit balance is too low to access the Anthropic API"

## What This Means
Your Anthropic account doesn't have enough credits to make API calls. You need to add credits to your account.

## How to Fix

### Step 1: Go to Anthropic Console
Visit: [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com)

### Step 2: Navigate to Billing
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your profile/account menu
3. Go to **"Plans & Billing"** or **"Billing"**

### Step 3: Add Credits
1. Click **"Purchase Credits"** or **"Add Credits"**
2. Choose an amount (usually minimum $5-10)
3. Complete the payment
4. Credits will be added immediately

### Step 4: Verify
- Check your credit balance in the dashboard
- It should show available credits
- Try generating again!

## Alternative: Free Trial
If you're new to Anthropic:
- Check if you have a free trial available
- Some accounts get free credits to start
- Look for "Free Trial" or "Welcome Credits" in your dashboard
